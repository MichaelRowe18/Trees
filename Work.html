Building Houses

How is work related to building houses?

By having multiple projects where we build homes, we are also creating jobs that could expand to more opportunities such as:

Improving skills
- The workers get the chance to work with new workmates, which they can learn and improve themselves from.
- At the same time they are mastering the skills they already knew.
Finding new positions
- Once again, by working with lots of new people on every project, they have the chance to find new projects or even better get new, more sustainable jobs.
